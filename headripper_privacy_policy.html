Headripper Chrome Extension — Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 10, 2025

Summary

  • No accounts collected. No analytics. No telemetry.
  • No servers used by the extension. Nothing leaves your browser except requests you make to Headspace.
  • No extension storage used. The extension does not save tokens, settings, or history.
  • Any content you choose to download saves directly to your device. You control the files.

What the extension accesses

The extension only touches what it needs to do the job and only when you trigger it.

  • Session cookie. Reads the hsngjwt cookie for https://my.headspace.com/ to verify you are signed in and to authenticate requests made by your browser to Headspace.
  • Active Headspace tab. Reads the current page location and minimal page data to find content identifiers.

This happens in memory. The extension does not persist this data.

What the extension stores

Nothing. The extension does not use chrome.storage and does not write files. The only files involved are the ones you choose to download and those are saved by Chrome to your chosen folder.

Data sharing

No data is shared, sold, or sent to third parties by this extension. Requests go from your browser directly to Headspace using your existing session.

Permissions and why they are needed

PermissionUse
cookies for https://*.headspace.com/* Read your hsngjwt cookie in memory so your browser can authenticate requests you initiate.
activeTab or tabs Know which Headspace tab you are working in to resolve content identifiers.
scripting scoped to https://my.headspace.com/* Run minimal scripts on Headspace pages to read content identifiers when you click the extension.
downloads (if enabled) Let you save content variants to your device using Chrome’s built in download flow.

Retention and control

  • The extension keeps no records to delete.
  • To revoke the session, sign out of Headspace or clear site cookies in your browser.
  • Remove the extension to fully remove its code and any temporary in memory data.

Security

The extension runs locally in Chrome’s extension sandbox and uses the least permissions required. No token is persisted by the extension. Do not use on shared or untrusted machines if you are signed in to Headspace.

Children

The extension is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the effective date will be updated. Continued use after a change means you accept the updated policy.
