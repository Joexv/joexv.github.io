Privacy Policy

Headripper Chrome Extension

Effective date: September 10, 2025

Summary

Headripper is a user tool that helps you browse your Headspace content and fetch available variants for personal use. The Chrome extension runs entirely on your device. It does not operate a cloud backend and it does not sell data.

  • No analytics. We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data.
  • No server storage. We do not send your account data to our servers because there are no servers.
  • Local only. Any tokens or lists the extension uses live in your browser storage on your device. You can clear them at any time.

Scope

This policy covers the Headripper Chrome extension. If you also use the optional desktop client, that app has its own local storage prompts and can store credentials in your OS keychain with your explicit consent. This page focuses on the extension.

What the extension accesses

The extension may read the following only to perform its core function and only after you initiate an action:

  • Headspace session cookie. Specifically the hsngjwt cookie scoped to my.headspace.com. This is needed to verify that you are signed in and to authenticate your own API requests to Headspace.
  • Page context. The URL of the active Headspace tab and minimal page data needed to locate content identifiers. The extension does not read arbitrary pages.

The extension does not collect your email address, payment information, or other profile data.

What the extension stores

  • Auth token. If you choose to save it, the hsngjwt value is stored in chrome.storage.local on your device. This storage belongs to your browser profile. You can clear it from the extension options or by removing the extension.
  • Local catalogs. Optional lists of categories, items, and available audio variants for faster browsing. These are stored locally in the same browser storage and can be cleared.

Chrome does not encrypt extension storage by default. Your operating system may protect the user profile directory. Do not use this extension on shared or untrusted machines.

Data sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your information. The extension makes requests directly to Headspace services using your own authenticated session. No third party receives your token from us.

Permissions and why they are needed

Permission Use
cookies for https://*.headspace.com/* Read your hsngjwt session cookie after you request authentication. This proves you are signed in and authorizes your requests to Headspace.
storage Save your local settings, optional token, and optional content catalogs for faster navigation.
activeTab or tabs Identify the current Headspace tab that you explicitly interact with so we can match the right session and content.
scripting restricted to https://my.headspace.com/* Inject minimal logic to read content identifiers or trigger authentication flow only on Headspace pages.
downloads (if enabled) Let you download content variants that your account has access to. Files save directly to your machine.

Retention and control

  • You can clear stored data from the extension options at any time.
  • You can remove the extension, which deletes its local storage.
  • You can revoke the session by signing out of Headspace or changing your password. That invalidates the hsngjwt cookie.

Children

The extension is not directed to children under 13 and should not be used by them.
